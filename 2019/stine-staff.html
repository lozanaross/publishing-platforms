This book shows how the patterns of our everyday life have changed over the past six centuries, by pairing paintings from the 15th through to the 19th century against amateur photographs from the present day. Everything in this book is generated by various online tools relying on computer intelligence. The printed book can be purchased and previewed on Blurb; the e-book can be previewed and downloaded for free via the Internet Archive; a screencast of the e-book can be viewed on YouTube. You can also visit the book's Instagram account and leave a comment on your favourite image pair here: @womanwithacat_project.
