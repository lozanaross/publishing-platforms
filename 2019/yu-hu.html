Yu Hu

'The Evolution of Chinese Memes' tells the story of the most popular memes in China in recent years. It introduces meme sources from Chinese pop culture, provides links to the contexts within which these memes circulate, and looks at the positive and negative effects that communicating in memes can bring to people's lives. The book is available as a Print-on-demand publication via Blurb and an interactive edition can be previewed and downloaded from the Internet Archive.
