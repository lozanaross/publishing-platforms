Manon Veyssiere

Girlhood 2.0 aims at rendering the experience of growing up as a teenage girl in the 2000’s, through the association of animated GIFs and classic poetry by female authors. GIFs are taken from Gifcities and various Skyblogs; poems are taken from the Project Gutemberg and Public Domain Poetry.
🦋 Inspirations behind this project are the work of the group Salut c’est cool and Jenny Odell's talk “How To Do Nothing” at Eyeo 2017.
