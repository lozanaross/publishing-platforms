Carolina Amane

This book focuses on viral videos of life hacks, office hacks and kitchen hacks. It highlights the number of views and followers these videos have and the effects these videos have on the viewers. All the videos from this book are found on Youtube.com and have been made into gifs and short videos. The printed book can be purchased and previewed on Lulu and the e-book can be downloaded for free via the Internet Archive.
