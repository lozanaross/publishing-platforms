Alice Woodward

At a time of growing ambition to explore the far reaches of our solar system and venture into interstellar space, this photography book collates some of the most breathtaking images captured by NASA. Moving outward from Earth, exploring the manmade satellites and celestial objects along the way, BEYOND provides a snapshot of our place in the universe. The printed book can be previewed and purchased on Blurb; the ebook can be previewed and downloaded for free via the Internet Archive; a screencast of the ebook and flip through of the print book can be viewed on YouTube; and all graphics used in the publication are available on Wikimedia Commons.
