Mariia Tokar

This book examines some of the most common misleading food labels and how to avoid being tricked by them. Nowadays, a lot of consumers have a hard time understanding nutrition labels, so this project is aiming to help them make smarter supermarket choices. The printed book can be purchased on Lulu. The ebook can be previewed and downloaded for free via the Internet Archive. A video preview of the printed book is available on YouTube.
