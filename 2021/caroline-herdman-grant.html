Caroline Herdman-Grant

The internet and way we use it is always evolving, from early static pages during the 90s to social media today. This project aims to showcase examples of the changing web through snapshots of selected sites, documenting shifts in internet usage from the emergence of early sites to the growth of social media and user-generated content. All images and text within the book have been collated using online content and snapshots generated from both live and archive sources. The book is available as a print-on-demand publication via Lulu and as both .epub and .pdf files on the Internet Archive. This is not a comprehensive history, more a glance at how things change.
